# Simple Team - Commander & Worker

A minimal 2-member team for testing and debugging team features.

## Team Structure

**Commander** (Team Lead)
- Coordinates the work
- Assigns tasks to the worker
- Reviews and validates completed work

**Worker**
- Executes assigned tasks
- Reports results to commander

## Example Workflow

1. User asks: "Create a hello world function in Python"
2. Commander assigns the task to Worker
3. Worker creates the function and saves it
4. Commander reviews and validates the work
5. Team reports completion to user
